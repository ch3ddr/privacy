# Privacy Policy – Camping Village

**Last updated:** June 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the **Camping Village** app ("we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use it. Camping Village is a festival guide: it shows the camp and stage lineup, an interactive map, and lets you build a personal plan of the sets you want to see.

## Information We Collect

For festival-goers, the app collects **no personal information** and requires **no account or sign-in**.

- **Your personal plan / favourites** are stored **only on your device** and never leave it.
- **App preferences** (such as language) and an **offline copy of the lineup** are cached **locally on your device** so the app keeps working without a connection.

## Staff / Admin Access

Authorised organisers can unlock editing with a **passcode**. The passcode grants permission to update the lineup; it is not linked to any personal profile, email address, or phone number. Lineup changes made by staff are saved to our backend (Supabase).

## Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect:

- Names, email addresses, or phone numbers from festival-goers
- Location data
- Camera, photos, or contacts
- Usage analytics, advertising identifiers, or crash reports sent to third parties

The app shows **no ads** and uses **no tracking technologies**.

## Network & Third-Party Services

- The app connects to **Supabase** to download the public festival lineup (camps, stages, set times). No personal data about you is sent. Supabase's privacy policy: [supabase.com/privacy](https://supabase.com/privacy).
- The interactive map renders from content bundled inside the app.
- The app may load fonts from **Google Fonts** (`fonts.gstatic.com`); no personal data is sent with these requests.
- Some buttons may open an external website or app-store page in your browser. Once you leave the app, the destination's own privacy policy applies.

## Data Storage & Retention

The lineup is public festival content stored on Supabase. Your personal plan and preferences live **only on your device** and are removed when you delete the app.

## Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
